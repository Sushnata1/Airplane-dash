Help

The aim of this game is to hover on the coloured squares(on PCs) Or tap them (on mobile devices) to get 1 point. If a white square is tapped or hovered, the game is over. Press on dark red box to change your name. If the history size becomes too large, Press on CLEAR on the game over page.
